Cashback Policy

Understanding how our cashback program works

Earning Cash Back

To earn cash back, you must click through from PetbizOS to the retailer's website and complete your purchase in the same browsing session. Cash back rates vary by retailer and may change over time.

Pending Period

Cash back earned will appear as "pending" in your account until the retailer's return period has passed. This typically takes 30-90 days depending on the retailer's policy.

Cash Back Availability

Once the pending period has passed and the retailer confirms the transaction, your cash back will become available for withdrawal.

Minimum Withdrawal

The minimum amount required to request a withdrawal may vary by payment method. Please check your account settings for current minimums.

Exclusions

Cash back may not be available on certain purchases, including:

  • Gift card purchases
  • Purchases made with certain promotional codes not listed on PetbizOS
  • Returns, exchanges, or cancelled orders
  • Purchases made outside the tracking window

Missing Cash Back

If you believe cash back is missing from your account, please submit a claim within 90 days of your purchase. We'll investigate with the retailer on your behalf.

Account Termination

PetbizOS reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts that violate our terms of service or engage in fraudulent activity.

Questions?

If you have questions about our cashback policy, please refer to our How It Works page or contact support.
