Cashback Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

How Cashback is Earned

Cashback is earned when you complete a qualifying purchase through PetBizos. To earn cashback:

  • Click an "Activate Cashback" button on PetBizos
  • Complete your purchase on the brand's website in the same browser session
  • The transaction must be completed within 24 hours of clicking the button
  • Cookies and JavaScript must be enabled in your browser

Cashback Rates

Cashback rates vary by brand and may change without notice. The rate shown at the time you activate cashback is the rate that will apply to your purchase, subject to the brand's terms and conditions.

Tracking Period

Purchases typically appear in your account as "pending" within 48-72 hours. If a purchase doesn't appear within 7 days, please contact our support team.

Confirmation Period

Cashback moves from "pending" to "confirmed" status within 30-60 days after purchase. This waiting period allows time for returns and ensures the transaction is valid.

Payment

Once cashback is confirmed, you can request payment through available methods (PayPal, bank transfer, or gift cards). Minimum payout thresholds may apply.

Returns and Cancellations

If you return a product or cancel an order, the associated cashback will be removed from your account. If the cashback has already been paid out, you may be required to repay it.

Exclusions

Cashback may not be available on:

  • Purchases made with gift cards or store credit
  • Tax, shipping, and other fees (unless specifically stated)
  • Purchases that violate the brand's terms of service
  • Fraudulent or abusive transactions

Account Suspension

PetBizos reserves the right to suspend or terminate accounts that engage in fraudulent activity, abuse of the service, or violation of our terms.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this cashback policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Questions

If you have questions about this cashback policy, please contact us at support@petbizos.com.
