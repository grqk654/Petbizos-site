Frequently Asked Questions

When will I receive my cash back?

Cash back is typically available for withdrawal 30-90 days after your purchase, once the retailer's return period has passed.

Is there a minimum withdrawal amount?

Yes, the minimum withdrawal amount varies by payment method. Check our Cashback Policy for details.

Can I use coupons or promo codes?

In most cases, yes! However, some retailers may not allow cash back on purchases made with certain promotional codes. Always check the specific brand's terms.
