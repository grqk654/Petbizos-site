How It Works for Partners

  1. Join Our Network: Sign up for our partner program and set your commission rate.
  2. We Promote Your Brand: We feature your brand on our website and promote your products to dog owners.
  3. Customers Shop: When customers activate cashback and shop at your site, we track their purchases.
  4. You Pay Commission: You pay us a commission only on completed sales. We share this with customers as cashback.
  5. Get New Customers: Benefit from increased traffic, sales, and customer loyalty.

Frequently Asked Questions

What commission rates do you require?

Commission rates vary by industry and product type. We work with you to set a rate that's competitive and beneficial for both parties. Typical rates range from 5% to 20% of sale value.

How are sales tracked?

We use industry-standard affiliate tracking technology with cookies and tracking pixels to accurately attribute sales to PetBizos referrals.

When do I pay commissions?

Commissions are typically paid on a net-30 or net-60 basis after the return period has passed and sales are confirmed.

Can I run my own promotions?

Yes! You can offer special deals and increased cashback rates to PetBizos customers to drive even more sales.

Is there a contract?

Yes, we have a standard partnership agreement that outlines terms, commission rates, payment schedules, and responsibilities for both parties.
